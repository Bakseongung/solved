- My solution
```
The `mean` function calculates the average. However,
if Michael Jordan’s annual salary is 10 billion won,
due to the pitfalls of the mean, using `mean` won’t give you the exact middle value.
In such cases, you can sort the data and then use the `median` function to find the middle value.
```

```
Mean is highly affected with Outliers, thus, in presence of outliers,
Median is a better option to use
(Median fails when number of outliers are more than 50% of the data)
As Skewness of the data increases (in a normal distribution),
Median keeps on becoming a better option to use.
As using Mean might result in incorrect results as extreme points might affect
the overall behaviour of data & might give false insights.
```
